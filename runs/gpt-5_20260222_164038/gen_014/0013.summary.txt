## Type of utopia
A **civic-infrastructure utopia** built around **practical governance, auditability, reversibility, and universal baseline security**. It is not “perfect people” but **systems that make decency routine**, with strong protections against coercion, hidden manipulation, monopoly power, and unsafe high-risk technology.

---

## Core values / operating principles (explicitly trained into culture and law)
- **“The floor follows you”**: a portable, guaranteed baseline of safety and resources independent of employers/landlords.
- **Exits are cheap**: leaving a job, service, platform, lease, or data arrangement is designed to be easy and non-punitive.
- **Reasons before rules**: every coercive or impactful decision must ship with a plain-language explanation, evidence, uncertainty, and stop conditions.
- **Change holds a rope**: nothing large gets installed without a funded plan and proven ability to undo it.
- **Keys turn together**: dangerous/high-leverage tools require multi-party activation representing builders, skeptics, and affected residents.
- **The commons pays the common**: wealth created by location, scarce privileges, and shared data returns to everyone.

---

## Baseline welfare (“the floor”) as a boring utility, not charity
Guaranteed entitlements available without moral scrutiny:
- **Housing**: a lockable room; basic dignity; no audition/dossier.
- **Privacy kill-switch**: a physically testable “dead switch” that disables cameras/mics.
- **Food**: simple, healthy staples; not conditional on performance; public kitchens teach skills without stigma.
- **Transit**: reliable, inexpensive access; designed so poverty never forces dangerous travel choices.
- **Power + connectivity** (“light and line”): minimum electricity and compute/communications sufficient to function civically (forms, appeals, learning).
- **Healthcare**: purchased and evaluated by **outcomes people care about**, not opaque billing or rushed appointments.
- **Income support**:
  - **Commons Dividend** paid weekly (unromantic, automatic).
  - Over time enables **refusal of bad bargains** (anti-coercion function).
  - **Sabbatical Years**: 1 in 7 years at half pay, no moral auditing.
  - **Rest Seasons**: months-long stabilization support after catastrophe, with a human case-buffer.

---

## Anti-coercion social design (power is prevented from becoming hostage-taking)
- Rapid escape support if a boss/landlord becomes abusive (“a van appears”; benefits/housing floor remains intact).
- **Portable benefits spine**: health/leave/retirement attached to the person, not controlled by employers.
- **Care work is credited**: eldercare, childcare, recovery support, community help can be stamped and banked like formal labor.
- **Cooling-off rules** for major loans/subscriptions; neutral explanation of terms before commitment.

---

## Economy and property: common value is captured for the public
Key mechanisms funding the dividend and civic goods:
- **Land is leased, not sold**: individuals own buildings/improvements, but location value returns to the public via rent.
- **Dormancy rent**: keeping storefronts/lots empty is costly, pushing property into use rather than speculative withholding.
- **Scarce privileges are time-limited and priced**: curb space, port berths, ferry slips, spectrum, festival sound windows, low orbits, etc.
  - **Open auctions**, thin rules, renewal conditional on good behavior.
  - Revenue flows into:
    - a per-person dividend
    - local reinvestment funds (“Place Pots”)
    - long-term shared investments (“Earth Dues”: ecosystems, maps, tutors, safety compute)
- **Windfall-sharing contracts**: frontier infrastructures (reactors, labs, fast compute) must return excess gains to commons and watchdogs.
- **Open “pattern” economy**: publishing useful designs/repairs earns ongoing **Pattern Dividend** when widely adopted (pay for use, not hoarding).
- **Data trusts**: crowd-generated value (health, transit, etc.) monetized as aggregate answers, not raw row-sales.
  - Royalties flow to contributors.
  - **Data portability and “data divorce”**: leaving means taking your data/social graph in standard formats.

## Work, skills, and production: competence over gatekeeping
- Traditional licensing replaced by **competence stamps**:
  - demonstrations (video), sensor logs, practical examinations
  - safety-focused, portable across regions via treaties
- **Robots as apprentices**: assist/haul/hold; not supervisory control systems.
- **Foundries and repair culture**: distributed workshops, pattern libraries, retrofits ready-to-install.
- **Measurement and replication funded**: incentives for “dull excellence” and verified improvements.

---

## Attention and persuasion: manipulation is permitted only with transparency and user control
- **Mandatory labeling of nudges**: if something steers attention/behavior, it must disclose purpose, evidence, conflicts, opt-out, complaint path in child-readable language.
- **Persuasion budget**: individuals set how much influence they allow (like a thermostat).
- **Attention levy / rebate**:
  - products that measurably worsen loneliness/brittleness pay
  - designs that improve wellbeing get rebates
- **Interoperability is law**: messages and social ties move with the user.
- **Opacity limits at scale**: large firms must open interfaces or books.
- **Anti-cartel “Challenger Fund”**: citizens can trigger rapid creation of a worker-owned competitor using open designs and unlocked capital/staff.

---

## Governance: decisions must be inspectable, contestable, and reversible
- **Reasons Receipts** attached to government decisions:
  - aims, data, consulted parties, funding influences, success criteria, stop conditions, and a funded stop plan
  - disclosures if AI assisted and when it stopped learning
- **Second Voice panels**: randomly selected citizen mirror-bodies publish dissent/additions for major decisions.
- **Promise Cards** before projects begin:
  - measurable commitments (cost, timeline, beneficiaries, named risks)
  - includes explicit triggers for stopping
- **Civic forecasting exchange** (limited domain): public bets/positions on whether promises are met; agencies hold exposure to their own claims.
- **Truth Bonds** for leaders: if outcomes miss stated ranges badly, compensation flows to those harmed by overconfidence.
- **Policy recall / pull-tabs**: rules have serial numbers; enough credible harm reports can auto-pause a policy.
- Independent **Safety Board** writes in practical, mechanic-like terms; publishes fixes, accountability, and make-good plans.
- **Harm Autopay**: if civic/medical experiments or invited risks harm participants, compensation is fast and default (not lawsuit-first), with human support.

---

## High-risk technology governance: multi-key control, audits, and “cutlinks”
Applies to AI, compute, cranes/money-moving code, biotech, etc.
- **Counted keys** required to activate dangerous systems:
  - **Maker** (understands and advocates use)
  - **Skeptic** (paid to doubt, test, red-team)
  - **Ward** (represents those living with consequences)
- Keys must be held by different people; actions logged outside key-holders’ control.
- **Compute passports**: high-end chips/models have signed provenance (“I did this then”).
- **Run registration** for training above a risk threshold.
- **Wardens** monitor goal drift/misalignment.
- **Audible cutlink**: physical/semi-physical ability to sever connections to “sharp compute”; practiced via public drills (normalizing emergency procedures).
- **Biotech in glass**:
  - verification of “no bad bugs” without exposing customer identities (visible math)
  - face-to-face stamps for high-end gear
  - gene drives require public deliberation, escrowed reversal plans, independent monitoring

---

## Built environment: fast permitting for safe patterns; materials transparency; climate adaptation
- A **pattern library** of proven building types (courtyard walk-ups, timber mid-rises with two stairs, corner shops with housing above).
- **Fast permits** if building stays within tested envelopes; still feasible to deviate with engineering and simulation.
- **Retrofit kits** pre-developed: garage-to-flat, insulation/ventilation, heat pumps designed for older buildings.
- **Materials passports** for components (origin, composition, reuse instructions).
- **Flood-adaptive design**: floodable ground floors with safe storage above; managed retreat when necessary with dignity.
- Water and nutrient pollution tracked publicly (simple metrics children learn).
- Nature given institutional standing: eel runs/fox paths have trustees with votes; a “Sentience Atlas” evolves law toward nonhuman consideration.

---

## Social infrastructure and everyday culture
- **Welcome Houses** at transit nodes:
  - showers, lockers, tea, chargers, basic care, translation help, simple bureaucracy without traps
- **Soft Evenings**: scheduled citywide reduction in stimulation (amber/slow screens), while essential services remain; workshops shift to quieter modes.
- **Mapped silence vs stimulation zones**: conflicts are solved by providing different spaces rather than moral argument.
- **Public drills as community events**: emergency readiness normalized with festivals, food, music.
- **Children’s rights card**:
  - guaranteed literacy/numeracy/critical testing skills
  - hands-on making/repair exposure
  - mobility across neighborhoods
  - right to speak with an outside trusted adult
  - limited right to leave briefly if they can explain why
  - default credibility: “We believe you.”

---

## What “utopia” means here (defining characteristics)
- **High trust produced by verification**, not by romantic ideals.
- **Low humiliation systems**: benefits, healthcare, help, and corrections are designed to avoid moral judgment.
- **Resilience through reversibility**: rollback is funded, rehearsed, and culturally normal.
- **Democratic constraint of high-leverage tech**: no single actor can deploy tools that reshape society.
- **Prosperity without rentier domination**: land value, scarce privileges, and network/data value are socialized via dividends and local reinvestment.
- **A “boring” reliability ideal**: trains on time, lights stay on, promises have ranges, and failure modes are explicit and compensated.
