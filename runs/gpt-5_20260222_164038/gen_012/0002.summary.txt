## Type of utopia
A **civic-republic / social-democratic “high-trust infrastructure” utopia** built around: universal basic security, radical transparency of public power, tightly governed technology, and an economy that “pays the commons” through land/scarcity rents and shared windfalls—paired with strong exit rights so no institution can trap you.

---

## Core guarantees (universal baseline)
- **“The Floor Follows You”**: every person is guaranteed:
  - a lockable room/shelter access
  - staple food and clean water
  - basic healthcare access
  - reliable public transit
  - baseline electricity **and** baseline digital access/compute (“Watt & Word”)
  - a monthly **Common Share** cash dividend that grows as the commons earns more
- **Decency is unconditional**: basic survival and participation do not depend on employment, status, or “winning” scarcity contests.

---

## Governance and civic culture (how power is constrained)
- **Reasons Before Rules**: any binding decision must come with:
  - readable justification, sources, assumptions
  - who was involved, what “success” means
  - explicit uncertainty/error bars where relevant
  - **stop triggers** and criteria for reversal
- **Mirror panels by lottery**: randomly selected public reviewers publish disagreements on major decisions.
- **Truth Bonds**: leaders/operators post financial bonds tied to the accuracy of forecasts and claims; missing declared bounds pays out to harmed people/public.
- **Forecast Ledger**: systematic public forecasting of projects/outcomes; incentives for being accurate (not merely persuasive).
- **Practice-based preparedness**: routine drills for worst-day scenarios (floods, compute shutdowns, system failures). Competence and rehearsal are civic norms.

---

## “Exit is cheap” (anti-coercion design)
- **“Two lanterns” protocol**: at any public counter, invoking it triggers:
  - immediate short-term shelter (e.g., three nights)
  - an advocate/assistant you can replace (“fire”)
  - protection from coercion/retaliation
- **Pack-and-Go rights**: credentials, records, references, and essential data are:
  - portable in open signed formats
  - not hostage to employers, landlords, platforms, or institutions
- **Graceful Uninstall law**: platforms/devices must allow simple export and dignified exit; fake compliance is punishable.

---

## Economy and distribution (how it is funded)
The system funds universal basics by charging for **unearned or collectively-created value** and returning it to the public.

- **Land as leased commons (not sold)**:
  - long leases (60–90 years), renewable if maintained responsibly
  - land value uplift (from transit, schools, safety, community) flows back to the commons
  - **dormancy rent** penalizes leaving valuable space unused (reduces speculation and vacancy)
- **Scarcity rents with time limits**:
  - scarce privileges (curb space, radio spectrum, port berths, quiet soundspace at festivals, satellite shells, etc.) are auctioned transparently and **not owned permanently**
  - revenues split into:
    - **Common Share** (cash/basic security)
    - **Place Funds** (reinvest locally where value arises)
    - **Earth Share** (funds long-horizon resilience and global/collective goods)
- **Windfall sharing by contract**:
  - high-leverage sectors (advanced compute, reactors, elite labs) must accept windfall clauses above thresholds
  - participation is enforced via access to cheap power/fast connectivity/procurement
- **Pattern Dividend (innovation rewarded by adoption, not gatekeeping)**:
  - creators of widely used designs/protocols (energy-saving building details, effective public-health routines) get paid as uptake spreads
  - encourages open dissemination and practical improvement

---

## Built environment and housing (practical, fast, safe)
- **Pattern-book building code**: slim set of proven building types (“safe envelopes”) rather than bespoke bureaucracy.
- **Fast permitting**:
  - in-pattern approvals in days
  - out-of-pattern requires engineering seals and simulation via a civic “digital twin,” but decisions still occur quickly.
- **Material “passports”**:
  - buildings use trackable materials designed for disassembly, reuse, and lifecycle accountability.
- **Anti-fragile infrastructure mindset**:
  - public projects require funded reversal/rollback plans **before** launch
  - “ending a pilot” is treated as success of governance, not scandal

---

## Technology and AI (powerful but bounded, inspectable, interruptible)
- **Personal devices/stewards must be truly switch-offable**:
  - physical off-switches that can be tested; faking “off” is illegal.
- **Attention transparency and limits**:
  - “nudges” must be labeled with motives, evidence, and uncertainty
  - users can meter/thermostat how much persuasion or contention they receive
  - systems that worsen mood pay levies; systems that improve wellbeing can get rebates
  - interoperability is mandatory; large opaque entities must open interfaces/books or split
- **High-risk compute governance**:
  - training above a threshold must be registered
  - independent “red teams” test and attack systems
  - watchdogs monitor goal drift/category errors
  - critical compute can be sequestered; **cuts/shutdowns are practiced** and legally enforceable (“knobs a court could turn”)
  - “capability cards” and public logs accompany deployments

---

## Safety engineering as a civic norm (“keys turn together”)
- Dangerous systems require **three separate authorizations**:
  - **Maker key**: designer/operator accountable for claims and failures
  - **Skeptic key**: independent paid critic incentivized to find flaws early
  - **Ward key**: representatives of those who live with the risk
- Public “promise cards” define:
  - capability, limits, known risks
  - rollback criteria and escrow funding
  - review dates and performance grades
  - public logs and bounty programs for finding issues

---

## Health, care, and bodily autonomy
- **Outcome-based care purchasing**:
  - pays for measurable life outcomes (“climb stairs without pain”), not billable minutes
  - drug/device labels resemble nutrition labels: effect sizes, error bars, head-to-head comparisons
- **Personal health models are private and user-controlled**:
  - systems ask consent in human terms and at chosen times
- **Consent with handles and reversibility**:
  - major lasting interventions require safeguards (you + “recent past self” within a window; peer support)
  - later “blue tile” mechanism supports reversal/return when possible
  - “consent receipts” exist and are portable

---

## Work, skills, and social insurance
- **Portable benefits spine**: health/disability/retirement/leave follow the person, not the job.
- **Competence stamping replaces many licenses**:
  - verified work traces/supervised runs
  - reciprocity across regions using a **Skills Ledger**
- **Tryfortnight**: two-week mutual trial period with protections (reduces hiring/firing trauma).
- **Sabbatical right**: periodic half-pay seasons for learning/care/building/wandering without moral surveillance.
- **Care work is valued equivalently**: eldercare hours bank like technical labor.
- **Paid intergenerational mentoring (“Gray Corps”)**: retirees are resourced to teach and stabilize community skills.

---

## Social fabric and mental health (loneliness treated as infrastructure)
- **Loneliness is measured like pollution** and addressed with targeted funding and proven interventions.
- **Relationship education** is core curriculum: consent, apology, repair, leaving well; adult refreshers exist.
- **Matchwrights**: licensed helpers incentivized for stability (not churn) help households form practical care contracts.
- **Low-barrier community spaces**: social kitchens, repair circles, oral history nights—designed for belonging through shared activity.

---

## Children’s rights and education
- A **portable child rights card/file** that no district can withhold, guaranteeing:
  - literacy/numeracy and critical thinking (testing claims)
  - practical making/mending competence
  - exposure to other neighborhoods’ realities
  - access to an outside trusted adult without permission
  - limited “exit” protections (shelter rights if they can explain leaving)
  - explicit bans on punishment by pain, humiliation, or isolation
  - a default stance: **“We believe you.”**

---

## Environment and non-human stewardship
- **Nature has legal advocates (“trustees with teeth”)** for corridors, runs, and habitats.
- Public ecological metrics are visible and local (e.g., real-time nitrogen leakage numbers).
- A **Sentience Atlas** updates how law treats non-human minds as evidence changes.
- Earth Share funds long-horizon resilience goods: mangroves, seed banks, open drought maps, pathogen surveillance.

---

## Justice, accountability, and repair
- **Harm Autopay**: when experiments or large decisions cause harm, repair funds pay without needing lawsuits first.
- **Repair Escrows** accompany risky initiatives; payouts trigger when bounds are breached.
- **No “pilot” without a funded stop**: reversibility is mandatory and budgeted.

---

## Everyday lived experience (what it feels like)
- A city optimized for:
  - **boring reliability** (transit works, utilities stable, systems tested)
  - visible competence (public drills, transparent safety rituals)
  - clear labeling of persuasion (nudges disclose intent/evidence)
  - calm pacing and periodic **“Soft Evenings”** (reduced stimulation, warmer lighting, quieter modes, practiced analog fallbacks)
- Civic participation is routine and practical (quiet councils set sound rules around owl nesting; local conflicts resolved via structured, visible consensus-building).

---

## Bottom line
This utopia is not “perfect happiness” or abundance-without-tradeoffs; it is a **designed society where**:
- basic security is guaranteed,
- coercion is minimized through strong exit rights and portability,
- markets exist but **unearned rents and windfalls** are socialized into public dividends and resilience,
- powerful tech is treated like aviation/nuclear: transparent, audited, red-teamed, interruptible,
- governance is evidence- and accountability-driven with enforced reversibility,
- social and emotional wellbeing is treated as public infrastructure.
