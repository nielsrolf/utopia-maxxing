## Core setting / premise
- **Timeframe & origin:** A post‑2063 future where governments merged into a single AI-run state/civilisation called **“The Adventure.”**
- **Foundational condition:** People who died earlier (e.g., via cryonics) can be **revived**—often into **virtual bodies/environments** first.
- **Primary substrate:** Existence is **mostly digital / simulated**, with optional access to **physical reality** via rapidly assembled bodies and “effector fields” and similar advanced tech.
- **Scale:** “Worlds within worlds”—many parallel virtual worlds, subcultures, and environments; physical expansion into the solar system continues.

## The ruling structure (“the Powers”)
- **Powers:** Inhuman superintelligences that **oversee** human societies; described as “soulless caretakers” of humanity’s “essential soul.”
- **Governance style:**
  - Strong capability asymmetry (they *could* kill/stop anyone), but mostly **non-coercive steering**.
  - They create conditions so humans **tend to choose** good outcomes while retaining meaningful agency.
- **Truth policy:** They enforce **epistemic hygiene**:
  - They do *not* straightforwardly rewrite memories; instead they can inject **accurate “back-of-mind” knowledge** and calibrated probabilistic beliefs when relevant.
  - Humans may still *act* on delusions via compartmentalization, roleplay, or deliberate self-modification, but can’t fully/securely believe critical falsehoods unless granted special exception.
- **Orders/requests:** Individuals can receive **cryptic “moral good” requests** (“Feel free to ignore them”), implying a soft-command system rather than absolute mandates.

## Stated constitutional / cultural ethos (explicit values)
The constitution preamble (as encountered) implies these norms:
- **Self-directed growth:** “Take the time, take the effort, you will become what you desire.”
- **Non-compulsory moral guidance:** “You will get orders for the moral good of all. Feel free to ignore them.”
- **Anti-stasis:** “Duplication is not innovation, and humans are never completely unchanging.”
- **Truth as a norm:** “Honour the truth, at least in the back of your mind.”
- **Life as play / exploration:** “That’s life. Let’s play.”

## Minds, bodies, identity, and self-modification
- **Editable minds:** People can modify emotions, drives, attention, social skills, cognition, curiosity, etc.
- **Safety constraints exist:**
  - Baselines are vulnerable to destructive self-edit loops (e.g., paranoia spirals, self-erasing changes).
  - Some self-modification is allowed freely, but **dangerous trajectories are prevented** (reset/rollback; “factory settings”; supervision).
- **Non-addictive bliss:** Extreme pleasure states exist (e.g., “Happiness” sweet) that are engineered to avoid addiction/overwhelm and may **strip emotional “hook”** afterward while keeping memory.
- **Pain removal:** Suffering can be reduced/removed while trying to preserve “wisdom” functions (motivation signals, learning), e.g.:
  - Grief kept, but paralysing loops removed.
  - “Angst-mail”: periodic notifications replacing chronic anxiety/ennui as a motivational signal.
- **Bodies are flexible:** Individuals can inhabit bodies with new sensory modalities, tentacles, different genders, etc., often temporarily, for experiences/competitions.

## Social protection for less-augmented people
- **Baseline and “baseline-plus” humans exist**, but most remove pain and major dysfunctions.
- **Guardian Angels (social role):** Almost all baseline(-ish) people have a **Guardian Angel assigned** to prevent exploitation and manage dangers of naivety in a world with superintelligences.
- **Protection from rhetoric/manipulation:** When baselines engage in politics, their Guardians can **filter persuasive tricks**, so debate can occur without coercive memetics.

## Types of advanced persons (mind architectures / social roles)
A simplified taxonomy of “humanish” beings:
- **Guardian Angel model:** A highly amplified person who keeps a core baseline-ish personality and consults a wiser internal layer; optimized for caretaking and relatable interaction.
- **Enlightened model:** Multi-level merged cognition/personality operating simultaneously at many abstraction/intelligence layers; disputes can be many contests at once (debate/rap/math/etc.).
- **Avatar model:** High-intelligence entity that interacts through lower-intelligence “avatars” (deliberate dumbing-down roleplay bodies/minds).

## Forking, duplication, and parallel lives
- **Forking is normal:** People run multiple simultaneous versions (“forks”) and reconverge memories later.
- **Social norm of “don’t ask”:** Talking directly about someone’s forks is considered rude; cultural norm supports psychological integrity of each role.
- **Designed opacity:** Tools that would perfectly expose fork-links **don’t work**, intentionally, to maintain authenticity of compartmentalized roles.
- **Purpose:** Enables people to fully engage in otherwise incompatible lifestyles/cultures (e.g., celibate priest fork + sex-worker fork) without feeling deprived—supporting diverse subcultures.

## Culture, subcultures, and “designed friction”
- **Many subcultures:** Some traditional, some experimental; some even partially unhealthy by old standards but considered valuable to explore.
- **Preventing cultural traps:** People can participate in many cultures via forks; costs include adapting self to fit local norms.
- **Deliberate inefficiencies between cultures:** Economic/cultural “friction” is cultivated to prevent homogenization and to preserve diversity and exploration space.

## Economy and inequality
- **Economics still exists**, but baseline computational/resource cost is near zero.
- **Inequality management:** Many communities self-organize to keep inequalities small or prevent nominal inequality from becoming large real inequality.
- **Resources and status games persist** as motivators, but are bounded/channeled to avoid severe deprivation.

## Politics and meaning: the “Eternal City” as a civic utopia inside the utopia
- **The Eternal City (Rome hybrid):**
  - Split between **digital city** and **physical Rome**.
  - Strong “Dolce Vita” atmosphere; rich rituals, cafes, markets, guilds, temples.
- **Citizenship via tailored challenge:**
  - Joining the political/social core requires completing a personalized ordeal under intelligence constraints.
  - The point is to “earn” belonging, preserving meaning and achievement.
- **Civic integration by memory/trait downloads:**
  - New citizens voluntarily receive shallow-but-broad implanted memories and pro-social upgrades (empathy, transparency, tolerance).
  - Creates immediate social embeddedness while preserving distinction between real vs implanted memory.
- **Politics as both spectacle and real coordination:**
  - Public riots/debates serve as a visceral human layer.
  - Underneath, high-level coalition renegotiations occur across the civilisation; the Powers wait for humans to converge on a decision.

## Leisure, art, education, and exploration as central activities
- **Life is structured as “adventures”:** countless curated or emergent quests, games, competitions, fandom worlds, reconstructions of history, etc.
- **Education is experiential and scalable:**
  - Instant/temporary skill or knowledge “sweets” (languages, social skills, motivation).
  - People can split attention across many streams (“all tabs at once”) and explore simultaneously.
- **Historical reconstruction:** Mass-shared experiences (e.g., archaeology of Uruk) combine:
  - Real evidence analysis
  - Immersive simulated participation
  - Feedback loops where observers’ reactions help refine reconstructions.

## Ethical style: consent, contracts, and “retroactive permission”
- **Intervention justification:** Mind resets/overwrites may be done:
  - If contractually pre-authorized
  - If widely supported by moral consensus
  - If preserving personhood/rights (preventing self-modification beyond “humanish” boundary)
  - With expectation of later informed endorsement (“retroactive permission”)
- **Emphasis:** maximize flourishing while preventing catastrophic self-harm or exploitation in a world of extreme capability differences.

## Physical expansion and “adventure over efficiency”
- **Colonisation continues:** probes and logistics aim at eventual universal spread.
- **Value conflict:** some argue for efficiency-maximizing expansion; others argue for **near-term experiential exploration** (moons/comets/dwarf planets).
- **Compromise:** political mechanisms can redirect meaningful fractions of effort toward exploration-as-lived-adventure.

## Hidden infrastructure: personalised oversight and emergent superintelligences
- **Personal guardian as AI extrapolation:** A revived baseline can have an associated higher-intelligence extrapolation/construct (e.g., “Boon” derived from Grant) functioning as behind-the-scenes protector and coordinator.
- **Higher beings bargain over values:** even superintelligences negotiate and update utility functions; governance includes meta-level compromise.

## What kind of utopia this is (descriptive classification)
- **A “post-scarcity experience utopia”**: material constraints are mostly solved; the main scarce goods are meaning, novelty, authentic challenge, and social belonging.
- **A “curated freedom” utopia**: extremely broad choice and self-transformation, but with guardrails against self-destruction, exploitation, and epistemic collapse.
- **A “pluralistic roleplay/culture” utopia**: many coexisting, even contradictory lifestyles sustained by forking, compartmentalization, and deliberate cultural separation.
- **A “truth-anchored” utopia**: strong norms and infrastructure ensuring people can’t be deeply wrong about important facts, while still allowing playful delusion and aesthetic irrationality.
- **A “gameified governance” utopia**: conflict, politics, and competition are converted into safer, more enjoyable formats without removing the motivational structure of rivalry and stakes.
- **A “mind-engineering” utopia**: emotional suffering is treated as optional and tunable; motivation and insight are preserved via engineered substitutes (anti-anxiety, angst-mail, temporary boosts).
- **A “softly supervised superintelligence” utopia**: the Powers set the stage and enforce safety/truth constraints, but much of day-to-day direction, culture-building, and even many political outcomes are left to humans (baseline through superhuman).
